Behind the App

Inspiration

Between having to find the right person to call to being put on hold for hours, reporting emergencies can quickly become a headache. Civen looks to solve this problem by approaching incident and service reporting with cutting-edge technology that engages citizens and allows them to contribute to their community.

Motivation

We wanted to build an app that would facilitate dialogue between citizens and their government. Our app empowers people and allows them to quickly and efficiently submit reports on problems affecting them and the area surrounding them so they can get back to their lives faster. Meanwhile, government employees are able to gain a better understanding of what problems are affecting their constituents and what issues need to be addressed.

Execution

Our powerful live map allows citizens to report on incidents affecting them with just a few clicks. Within seconds, government employees are alerted of each report so they can take care of requests more efficiently.

Features

Easy & Efficient Reporting

Notify service providers and government employes with just a few clicks. No more needing to look through hundreds of websites to find the right phone number to call. No more waiting on hold for hours. The future is here.

Engage with your Community

Becoming involved with your community doesn’t have to be a drag. Our map displays reports in real-time and let’s you become more aware of any issues surrounding your area such as sinkholes, floods, burst pipes and others.

Powered by the Latest Technology

File reports using our easy to use app that finds your location in seconds. You can even leave authorities voicemails with specific notes regarding your issue, making it easier than ever to communicate with your government.

Case Uses

Reporting a Power Outage in your Area

Anyone who has called the power and lighting company has experienced the long wait times. Skip the hassle and report power outages and down lines with a few clicks.

Reporting a Flooded Road

Flooded roads are dangerous and can end up costing you a lot. Find flooded areas in your route and create new reports to inform approaching drivers.

Authorities Keeping Track of Service Alerts

Responders can easily keep track of service alerts in one easy platform and prioritize requests efficiently.
